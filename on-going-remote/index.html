Going remote

08 March 2018

In February this year I accepted an offer for a full time remote working position as a software developer for a leading logistics company and I'm very excited about it. Working with micro-services and messaging systems is something I've always wanted to gain experience in as seems interesting and something that I haven't done before so there should be plenty of learning opportunities.

As well as adapting to a new working environment I also have the challenge of getting up-to speed on a whole bunch of new systems, a new team and a new architecture to adapt to so it's going to be a challenging few months ahead.

It’s going well so far with it’s own unique challenges and one of the most important lessons I’ve learnt so far is that it’s far better to over communicate! It's very different being detached from the office and working from your own space.

I did a fair bit of research on working remotely. It’s certainly not for everyone, but so far I’ve taken to it like a duck to water and I’m looking forward to the future and updating my blog with the experiences and lessons learnt.
